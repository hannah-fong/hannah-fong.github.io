Photography


From a young age, I loved taking pictures at any given moment. I loved how I could capture a moment in time, then look back at that moment as many times as I would like. The first camera I ever bought was a sea green Nikon Coolpix point-and-shoot camera back in 2007. I received my DSLR in high school and I've been excited to use it every possible chance since. More recently, my focus is outdoor portraits, but I still love to shoot nature and street life.


Please enjoy a few of my photographs, and check out my website!
