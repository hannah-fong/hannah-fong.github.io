Calligraphy

I've always been a handwriting fanatic. Growing up throughout middle school and high school, I basically obsessed over having typewriter-like handwriting. I stumbled upon handlettering and calligraphy almost two-and-a-half years ago on Instagram and was instantly hooked. Fast forward to today, brush calligraphy is one of my most favorite and satisfying hobbies! My favorite brush pens include Tombow Fudenosuke, Zig Dual Brush, and brush pens from Daiso (they're $1.50 each!).
